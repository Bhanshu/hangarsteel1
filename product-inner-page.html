steel hangar

Well built aircraft hangers will help protect your plane from the natural elements. And are also a cost effective way to protect your aviation long term investment.steel.

Modern airplane hangars use plans with designs that can include living quarters, offices, shop areas for repairs and many other designs. Using practical ideas and plans to customize your aircraft buildings the way you want are vital during the planning stage. There are many airplane hanger building types and options available that can be incorporated into todays metal designs for plane hangars. It is important to know what are the options and design features you desire during the planning stages for your new airplane hangars. Design and construct the hangar that best suits your specific needs and budget, by having all of your requirements met.

We design, manufacture and install manual or electro-mechanical steel framed, single skin or double skin insulated doors, with roller bearing, machined steel wheels running on ground rails. These can be ordered with a REIDsteel hangar, or ordered individually for an existing structure.
